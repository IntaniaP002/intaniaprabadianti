  • Why Golf is Fun?
    • Golf courses come in all different shapes and sizes. Most have 18 holes, and at each hole the golfer takes a drive or stroke in order to move the ball around the course to the next hole. The aim is to go from tee to green as quickly as possible, with as few strokes.
  • Teeing Area
    • Tee box is where the holde begins. When golfing, you will tee your ball between the two markers at each tee box.
  • Out of Bound
    • It is the area outside the fairway.
  • Fairway
    • The fairway generally measures 30 to 50 yards between the tee box and the green, simply, it's the body of the golf court and it has tightly trimmed grass.
  • Rough
    • The rough surrounds the fairways. This component has longer grass than the fairway, which means the surface isn't ideal for hitting the ball from.
  • Hazard
    • A golf course have a water as well as bunker or sand in order to make the game more challenging for golfers
  • Putting Green
    • It's where each hole is located. It has a shorter grass than the fairway which provide the perfect surface for the ball to get in the hole
