  • Why Golf is Fun?
    • The scoring system in golf is different than that of most sports, where the higher score is the winner. In golf, success is determined by the lower score, or the fewest number of shots taken on the hole or in the round.
  • PAR
    • is attained when a golfer completes a hole in the number of strokes listed on the course scorecard.
  • BOGEY
    • is the term used to express a score of one shot over par. Accordingly, a score of two shots over par is a double bogey, three over par is a triple bogey, and four over par is a quadruple bogey.
  • BIRDIE
    • is a score one stroke less than par
  • EAGLE
    • is a score in which a golfer completes a hole in two fewer shots than par. In most cases, eagles occur on par-4 or par-5 holes.
  • HOLE IN ONE
    • A hole-in-one occurs when a golfer holes the ball on the tee shot.
